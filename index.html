Rules and scoring

Scoring

Guess exact score for each team correctly+3
Guess winner correctly, irrespective of score+2
Guess draw correctly, irrespective of score+2
Guess score of only one team correctly+1

Example: actual result 2-1

Prediction 2-1 Exact score both teams +3, winner correct +2 Total: 5 pts
Prediction 2-0 One team score correct +1, winner correct +2 Total: 3 pts
Prediction 1-0 Winner correct +2 Total: 2 pts
Prediction 2-2 One team score correct +1, wrong result Total: 1 pt
Prediction 1-1 Wrong result, one team score correct Total: 1 pt
Prediction 0-3 Wrong result, no team score correct Total: 0 pts

League rules

Prediction window Predictions open 72 hours before each match starts and lock at kickoff.
Knockout matches Knockout predictions open only after the admin updates both team names.
Extra time For knockout matches, the score after 120 minutes is used as the final score. If it is 1-1 after extra time and a team wins on penalties, the prediction result is still treated as a 1-1 draw. Penalty shootout goals are not counted.
Predictions privacy Other players can see your past predictions only after that match has started.
